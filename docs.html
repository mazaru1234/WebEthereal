FAQ

How is the final price determined?

After the brief we prepare an estimate broken down by stage. It's fixed in the contract — no surprise charges unless the scope changes.

Do you require upfront payment?

Yes: 50% at start, 50% on delivery. For large projects — milestone payments (30 / 40 / 30).

Do you deliver source files?

Yes. Figma files, code in Git, hosting and CMS access — everything is handed over after final payment.

Can I order design only?

Yes, design-only is available. We deliver Figma with components and a UI kit.
