FAQ

Common questions

Payment, delivery, and timelines — before we start

Payment and revisions

A 100% upfront payment is required to begin the project.

Once the project is completed, you will have one day to review the website and request any necessary revisions. All adjustments and corrections within the agreed project scope will be made free of charge.

Do you deliver source files?

Yes. Source code in Git, hosting access, and admin credentials — everything is handed over when the project is completed and accepted.

How do we start a project?

Message us on Telegram or X with your idea. We agree on scope, price, and timeline in the brief. Development starts after 100% upfront payment.

How long does development take?

It depends on complexity. Each service shows minimum timelines on this page and in Pricing — your exact schedule is confirmed in the contract before work begins.

What is included in the price?

Everything listed in your plan within the agreed scope: layout, development, testing, launch, and handover. Features outside scope are quoted separately before any extra work starts.

Do you offer maintenance and SEO?

Yes. Website maintenance — one-time or long-term support with discounts for ongoing clients. SEO promotion with flexible budgets, no minimum spend, and no unnecessary upselling.

Who works on my project?

A 100% professional team — no inexperienced freelancers fresh out of online courses. You work directly with the people who build your product.

Are prices fixed or can they change?

100% transparent pricing — no fake metrics, hidden fees, or unexpected charges after the contract is signed. The price in your brief is the price you pay for the agreed scope.
